1:Use indents for each curly brace, this is to make it easier to read!
2:When you type something like a bracket or curly brace type the next one!
3:c++ is case sensitive, so FJAY is different to FjAY.
4:White spaces are ignored so you can have any amount of space unless they keywords or strings.
5:Use two forward slashes to comment, only comment relevant things!
6:Remember to change comments to keep relevant.
7:You can use multi line comments with forward slash asterisk then asterisk forward slash to end.
8:Use descriptive variable names.
